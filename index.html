      Franchise History
      
        
      
          
        
      
        
      
          Archives the current season — final standings, every player's batting and pitching line, and the playoff champion —
          then resets stats, schedule, and playoffs for a fresh season. Teams, rosters, lineups, pitching staffs, and divisions all carry over.
        
      
        
      
        
      Import Past Seasons (CSV)
      
        
      
          
          
          
        
      
        
      
          Got seasons you played by hand before using the app? Fill in the template (one Batting row per player-season,
          one Pitching row per pitcher-season, optional Record and Champion rows per season) and import them here.
          They'll appear in History and count toward the Records book. Keep player spelling consistent so career totals connect.
